About David

The stories are dark because the easy answers are usually lying.

David Beers writes thrillers, horror, military science fiction, and psychologically intense fiction about people under pressure. The books are violent, strange, loyal, haunted, and rarely interested in pretending the world is cleaner than it is.

They are also, stubbornly, about redemption. Not the greeting-card kind. The expensive kind.
